Marble Love

TypeScript reimplementation of Marble Madness (Atari, 1984). Load your local ROM ZIPs (marble.zip and, when needed, atarisy1.zip). ROM data stays on this device and is never uploaded.

Tip: select marble.zip and atarisy1.zip together (Cmd/Ctrl-click). After loading, use the arrow keys to scroll the 64x64 tilemap viewport (Shift = 8x faster).
